NonGMO Verified

We decided to pass on using mutated crops and went with normal ones instead. We promised ourselves that we would uphold a standard of quality sourced raw materials. To ensure this promise our products are NonGMO Verified from the NonGMO Project.

Certified Vegan

We try to make sure that our products reflect and demonstrate a level of respect and compassion for all living things. Each individual ingredient has been tested and verified allowing us to be 100% Vegan Certified from Vegan Action.

Glyphosate Residue-Free

Did you know Glyphosate is the most used herbicide in the world? Herbicides contaminate soil and are toxic for both the environment and the human body. To ensure our products are Glyphosate-Residue Free we test our products with the Detox Project Company.

Keto Friendly

Kick carbs to the curb with our 1g of carbs per serving 0g of sugar oat milk. Jam packed with unsaturated + monounsaturated fats (the good kind) and anti-inflammatory properties.

Dairy Free

This drink DID NOT come from the lacteal secretion derived from the mammary glands of a postpartum female mammal.

0g of Sugar
What a sweet surprise.

No Added Sugar? Unsweetened? Do these labels really mean No Sugar? To avoid confusion, we decided not to sugar coat it and went with 0g of sugar instead.
